University partnerships and connected organisations

Partnerships

Higher York

A wide-ranging partnership between the City Council and higher education providers in York.

  • Higher York

White Rose University Consortium

A collaboration between York, Leeds and Sheffield - the three leading research universities in Yorkshire.

  • White Rose University Consortium

Worldwide Universities Network (WUN)

A world-wide collaborative alliance of 15 research-led universities of international standing.

  • Worldwide Universities Network

York Cares

Brings businesses, employees and community organisations together to make a positive impact on York.

  •  York Cares

Yorkshire Universities

A forum for Universities Colleges to work together to extend their contribution to the region.

  • Yorkshire Universities

Russell Group

The Russell Group represents 24 leading UK universities which are committed to maintaining the very best research, an outstanding teaching and learning experience and unrivalled links with business and the public sector.

  • Russell group

University trading companies

This is a list of University companies which offer external services; only those with web pages are listed here. For further details on University companies not in this list, or to find out about other services, please contact Research and Enterprise.

York EMC Services Ltd

York EMC Services (YES) Ltd is a subsidiary company of the University of York and is a leading provider of Regulatory Compliance Testing, Training, Consultancy & Research and Test Equipment.

  • York EMC Services

York Health Economics Consortium Ltd

The Consortium was established with the intention of providing economic support to organisations within the National Health Service.

  • York Health Economics Consortium

Heslington Studios

Heslington Studios is the trading part of the Department of Theatre, Film and Television.

  • Heslington Studios

York Electronics Centre

The York Electronics Centre was an industrial unit within the University of York, and operated as part of the Department of Electronics. The YEC is no longer operating.

  • York Electronics Centre

York Conferences Ltd

York Conferences is responsible for the management of conferences and events at Heslington campus and King's Manor, as well as the National Science Learning Centre.

  • York Conferences

York Science Park Ltd

York Science Park is currently home to more than 100 companies in the science and technology sectors.

  • York Science Park Ltd

York Sport

York Sports Village LLP operates the sports centre.

  • York Sport

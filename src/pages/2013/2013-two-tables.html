Sustainability targets

Here is a list of some of the headline targets set to deliver the sustainability strategy.

Carbon Management To meet HEFCE interim 2017 tergat of 29% for reduction in CO2 emissions from a 2005/6 baseline.
  To provide 10% of energy from renewable sources for refurbished and new build buildings on campus
Waste Management Improve recycling rates in bins acorss campus to 60% of total waste
  Reduce the quantity of food waste from catering units going into non-food waste collections to zero by the end of the year.

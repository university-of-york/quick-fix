Welcome

Welcome to Wentworth College Online Community or Wentworth Online for short. These College pages are for you if you are, or hope to be, one of the 500+ members of the distance learning community at York. On these pages you will find information relating to how the University can support you in your studies, both academically (Study section) and pastorally (Support section), and you can also access resources and links for areas which we feel may be of interest to you and you can contribute to the community via our Facebook page or blog(s) (Community section).

If you are new to this site and new to distance learning at York then we recommend you start by taking a look at the Welcome pages made specifically for you.

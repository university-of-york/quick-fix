Online Chat

Social Policy and Applied Social Science offer holders

  • Thursday 25 April 2013, 12-1pm
  • With Dr Liz McDermott (Admissions Tutor), Angela Chapman (Undergraduate Administrator) and Megan O'Kane (current student)

How to take part

Taking part is easy. Once the event begins, you will see a box appear in the event window. Simply type in your first name and your question and click submit. There may be a short pause whilst your question passes through moderation.

Note

This online event will be publicly available on the web, and therefore we strongly advise you not to post any personal information such as your e-mail address, applicant number or passport number. If you choose to do so, it is at your own risk. For issues relating to your personal circumstances or application, please contact us at ug-admissions@york.ac.uk or telephone 01904 324000.

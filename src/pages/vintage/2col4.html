Want to understand the policy process?

Develop your knowledge of social research methods and policy analysis. We'll help you find ways to shape social policy of the future.

  • BA (Hons) Social Policy
  • BA (Hons) Extended Degree

Want to become a social work professional?

Combining theory, practice and reflection, you will gain the knowledge, practical skills and understanding of social work on one of the most highly regarded programmes in the country.

  • BA (Hons) Social Work

Want to apply theory to the real world?

Explore how social, economic and political decision making affects people’s lives. Apply what you learn to address contemporary social issues.

  • BA (Hons) Applied Social Science
  • BA (Hons) AppSocSci - Children
  • BA (Hons) AppSociSci - Crime
  • BA (Hons) Extended Degree

Want a wider social science perspective?

The Department of Social Policy and Social Work delivers two programmes in collaboration with other Departments, drawing on expertise from across the University.

  • BA Criminology
  • BA Social and Political Sciences

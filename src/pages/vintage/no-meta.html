Getting Here

Alcuin College is situated at the North of the main campus (Heslington West site), south-east of York City Centre. Getting here is easy - we have great rail links, easy access to major road networks and short travel time to international airports.

Parking

There are limited parking spaces across Campus West and Campus East. The nearest large car park is Campus North. There are also some parking spaces for people with disabilities and short-term parking in bays on Alcuin Way, near the College Reception. See the University's parking pages for more information about parking on campus at the University.

Taxi

A taxi from York Station to The University’s Information Centre is approximately £8. This is the price quoted by the University of York’s preferred Taxi Company, Streamline, 010904 656565.

Buses

YorBus Number 66 and Unibus Number 44 route connects York Railway Station, York City Centre and the University. There is also free bus travel between the two main campus sites. These run every 10 minutes during the daytime and offer clubbers services during the night. 

Cycling

Bike storage is free at the University, with many bike racks around campus. Make sure you secure your bike with a lock to prevent theft.

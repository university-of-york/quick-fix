Alumni

Keep in touch with the Department of Archaeology, and find out what we are up to now! We invite all former undergraduate and postgraduate students of the Department of Archaeology and the Institute of Advanced Architectural Studies to sign up to our alumni contact list so you can stay up to date with the York Archaeology community.

Have you joined YorkSpace?

YorkSpace.net is the University of York's online community for alumni and friends, and it is the way we keep our alumni records up to date. We encourage all our alumni to sign up to the site for free! For more information about what you can do with YorkSpace, see our About YorkSpace page.

Social Media

Social media is one of the easiest ways to keep up to date with the Archaeology Department. You can follow us on Facebook, Twitter, Google+, and YouTube.

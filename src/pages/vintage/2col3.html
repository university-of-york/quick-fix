Message from the Head of Department

"Welcome to all of you. We are delighted you have chosen to study with us. Whatever course you have chosen, our aim is to provide a supportive learning environment to enable each of you to fulfil your potential. We have a dedicated group of staff, with years of experience between them, who are here to guide and support you through your studies. I truly believe we offer some of the best UK educational programmes in the health and social care field so good luck, work hard and I hope you thoroughly enjoy your experience of studying in the Department of Health Sciences at the University of York."

Professor Karl Atkin
